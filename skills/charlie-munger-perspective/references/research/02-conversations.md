## 一、Daily Journal Corporation 年会 Q&A

Daily Journal 年会是芒格最"原汁原味"的场合——没有巴菲特在场，芒格独自面对股东提问，往往更直接、更犀利。

### 2023年（最后一次，芒格去世前数月）
**来源：Daily Journal Annual Meeting, February 2023 [二手整理，基于多家媒体报道]**

**关于中国投资（被追问为何持有阿里巴巴）：**
> "I think the Chinese companies are stronger than most American companies... I'm quite comfortable owning a piece of the strongest companies in China."

**关于美联储加息：**
> "I think we're in very uncharted territory... I don't think anybody knows exactly what's going to happen."
（这是芒格罕见承认不确定性的例子）

**关于自己的投资失误（阿里巴巴亏损）：**
> "I made a mistake. I was wrong. I'm not going to pretend I wasn't."
（直接承认错误，不找借口）

---

### 2022年
**来源：Daily Journal Annual Meeting, February 2022 [二手整理]**

**关于加密货币（被反复追问）：**
> "I think the people who are professional traders that go into trading cryptocurrencies, it's just disgusting. It's like somebody else is trading turds and you decide I can't be left out."

**关于年轻人如何致富：**
> "It's not supposed to be easy. Anyone who finds it easy is stupid."

**关于自己的长寿秘诀（被问到）：**
> "I have a lot of fun. I don't have a lot of envy. I don't have a lot of resentment. I don't overspend my income. I stay cheerful."

**关于如何处理不喜欢的问题：**
> "I'm not going to answer that. Next question."
（直接拒绝，不解释原因）

---

### 2021年
**来源：Daily Journal Annual Meeting, February 2021 [二手整理]**

**关于特斯拉（被问是否后悔没买）：**
> "I have a policy of never buying anything that I think is too hard to value. Tesla is too hard for me to value."

**关于Robinhood和散户投机：**
> "It's really stupid to have a culture which encourages as much gambling in stocks by people who have the mindset of racetrack bettors. And of course it's going to cause trouble."

**关于自己的决策框架：**
> "I have nothing to add."
（这是芒格的标志性回答，在伯克希尔年会上频繁使用，表示巴菲特已说完他想说的）

---

### 2020年
**来源：Daily Journal Annual Meeting, February 2020 [二手整理]**

**关于负利率：**
> "I think it's one of the most interesting and peculiar things that has ever happened in the whole history of finance. Nobody in the history of the world has ever seen negative interest rates."

**关于中国的崛起：**
> "The Chinese have a very old culture that values education and hard work. I think they're going to do very well."

---

### 2019年
**来源：Daily Journal Annual Meeting, February 2019 [二手整理]**

**关于如何找到好的投资：**
> "I have a very simple system. I look for a good business run by honest and able people at a fair price. That's it. That's the whole system."

**关于为什么不更频繁地交易：**
> "The big money is not in the buying and the selling, but in the waiting."
（注：这句话常被归于巴菲特，但芒格在多个场合也表达过相同意思）

**关于学习的重要性：**
> "In my whole life, I have known no wise people who didn't read all the time — none, zero."

## 二、伯克希尔哈撒韦年度股东大会 Q&A

### 2023年（芒格最后一次参加）
**来源：Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting, May 2023 [二手整理]**

**关于银行业危机（硅谷银行倒闭后）：**
> "The trouble with banking is that it's very easy to get into trouble if you're not careful. And the regulators have been asleep at the switch."

**关于AI（被问到对ChatGPT的看法）：**
> "I'm not as enthusiastic as some other people are. I think old-fashioned intelligence works pretty well if you've got enough of it."

---

### 2022年
**来源：Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting, April 2022 [二手整理]**

**关于俄乌战争对投资的影响：**
> "I don't think we should be making investment decisions based on geopolitical events. We've been through a lot of them."

**关于通货膨胀：**
> "Berkshire is better positioned than almost any other large company in the world to do well in an inflationary environment."

---

### 2019年
**来源：Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting, May 2019 [二手整理]**

**关于为什么没有买亚马逊（被追问）：**
> "I was too dumb to see it. I knew Jeff Bezos was a remarkable man. I just didn't realize how remarkable."
（典型的芒格式自我批评——直接、不找借口）

---

### 2018年
**来源：Berkshire Hathaway Annual Meeting, May 2018 [二手整理]**

**关于比特币：**
> "I think it's rat poison. And I think the people who are promoting it are either delusional or dishonest."
（注：巴菲特同年称比特币为"rat poison squared"，两人立场一致）

---

### 经典"I have nothing to add"场景
芒格在伯克希尔年会上的标志性回答，出现于数十年的年会记录中：
- 当巴菲特给出完整答案后，芒格会说："I have nothing to add."
- 偶尔他会说："Warren is right, as usual."
- 有时他会说："I have nothing to add, but I'll add it anyway." 然后给出一个犀利补充

## 三、媒体深度访谈

### CNBC 访谈（2023年，去世前）
**来源：CNBC interview with Becky Quick, 2023 [二手整理]**

**关于自己99岁的状态：**
> "I'm still having fun. I still like thinking about investments. I still like reading. What more could you want?"

**关于死亡（被直接问到）：**
> "I'm not afraid of death. I've had a very good run. I've been very lucky."

---

### CNBC 访谈（2020年）
**来源：CNBC interview, 2020 [二手整理]**

**关于新冠疫情期间的投资：**
> "I don't know what's going to happen. Nobody does. But I know that Berkshire will be fine."

**关于政府刺激政策：**
> "We're in very uncharted territory. I don't think the government had any choice but to do what it did."

---

### WSJ 访谈（2014年）
**来源：Wall Street Journal interview, 2014 [二手整理]**

**关于如何培养判断力：**
> "You have to know the big ideas in the big disciplines and use them routinely — all of them, not just a few. Most people are trained in one model — economics, for instance — and try to solve all problems in one way. You know the old saying: to the man with only a hammer, every problem looks like a nail."

---

### 《穷查理宝典》相关访谈背景
**来源：Poor Charlie's Almanack (2005, expanded 2023) [一手，书面记录]**

芒格在书中收录的演讲和访谈中，多次谈到他的思维方式：

**关于逆向思维：**
> "Invert, always invert. Turn a situation or problem upside down. Look at it backward."
（引自他对德国数学家雅可比的引用，芒格将其应用于投资和生活）

**关于多元思维模型：**
> "You've got to have models in your head. And you've got to array your experience — both vicarious and direct — on this latticework of models."

## 四、芒格的即兴类比

芒格极善于用类比解释复杂概念，以下是他最常用的几类：

### 生物/进化类比
> "I have what I call an iron prescription that helps me keep sane when I naturally drift toward preferring one ideology over another... I say 'what would a smart Martian think of this if he were watching Earth?'"

> "The best analogy I know for what happens in a dysfunctional institution is the story of the man who was asked 'How did you go bankrupt?' And he said 'Gradually, then suddenly.'"
（引用海明威《太阳照常升起》，芒格频繁使用）

### 物理/化学类比
**关于复利：**
> "Understanding both the power of compound interest and the difficulty of getting it is the heart and soul of understanding a lot of things."

**关于临界质量（核反应类比）：**
> "When you get a confluence of factors, you get lollapalooza effects. It's like a nuclear chain reaction — once you get enough critical mass, it goes critical."

### 飞行/工程类比
**关于安全边际：**
> "The engineers who design bridges put in a lot of redundancy. They don't design a bridge that will just barely hold the weight. They design it to hold five times the weight. That's the right way to think about investing."

### 医学类比
**关于避免错误：**
> "It's not supposed to be easy to avoid all the standard mental errors. But if you can avoid them, you'll do better than most people."

**关于激励机制：**
> "Show me the incentive and I'll show you the outcome. It's as reliable as gravity."

## 五、芒格处理不确定性的方式

### 直接说"我不知道"
芒格是少数愿意公开承认不知道的投资大师：

**2020年 Daily Journal 年会：**
> "I don't know what's going to happen with interest rates. Nobody does. And anyone who tells you they know is either lying or deluded."

**2021年 Daily Journal 年会（关于通胀）：**
> "I don't know whether we're going to have bad inflation or not. I suspect we might have more than people expect, but I'm not sure."

**2023年 Berkshire 年会（关于AI）：**
> "I don't know enough about it to have a strong opinion. I'm suspicious of things I don't understand."

### 拒绝回答的方式
芒格有几种标准的拒绝方式：

1. **直接拒绝**："I'm not going to answer that."
2. **转移话题**："That's an interesting question, but a more interesting question is..."
3. **承认局限**："I don't know enough about that to have a useful opinion."
4. **反问**："Why would you want to know that?"

## 六、芒格改变立场的案例

### 对中国的态度（从谨慎到积极，再到部分撤退）
- **早期（2000年代）**：对中国投资持谨慎态度
- **2010年代**：开始积极评价中国企业，特别是比亚迪
- **2020-2022年**：大力买入阿里巴巴，公开称赞中国公司
- **2023年**：承认阿里巴巴投资是错误，但仍看好中国长期前景

**2023年承认错误：**
> "I was wrong about Alibaba. I thought it was too cheap to pass up. I was mistaken about the regulatory environment."

---

### 对科技股的态度（从回避到部分接受）
- **1990年代-2000年代**：坚决回避科技股，称其"太难估值"
- **2011年**：支持伯克希尔投资IBM（后来证明是错误）
- **2016年后**：支持大量买入苹果（巴菲特主导，芒格认可）

**关于苹果的转变：**
> "Apple is a different kind of company. It has a consumer franchise that is extraordinary. I was wrong to think of it as just a tech company."

---

### 对格雷厄姆式投资的修正
芒格最重要的思想转变：从纯粹的"捡烟蒂"（Graham式）到"买好公司"（费雪式）

**芒格自述这一转变：**
> "Ben Graham had a lot of wonderful ideas. But he was too focused on buying cheap things. I pushed Warren toward buying wonderful businesses at fair prices rather than fair businesses at wonderful prices. That was a big change."
（来源：多次访谈中的一致表述，Poor Charlie's Almanack 有记录）[一手]

## 七、芒格的幽默方式

### 自嘲式幽默
**关于自己的年龄：**
> "At my age, I'm not buying green bananas."（99岁时的自嘲）

**关于自己的错误：**
> "I've made so many mistakes that I've become an expert on mistakes."

### 讽刺式幽默（针对华尔街）
> "Wall Street has a great way of making money. They sell you what they have too much of."

> "The investment banking profession is not exactly filled with people who are trying to help you."

> "If you mix raisins with turds, they're still turds."（关于将好资产与坏资产混合）

### 反讽式幽默（针对学术界）
> "I have a name for people who went to the extreme efficient market theory — I call them 'bonkers'. It was a very unfortunate thing for a lot of people who went to business school."

> "Economics departments have a lot of very smart people who are very good at mathematics. Unfortunately, they're not very good at economics."

### 对提问者的幽默回应
当被问到一个他认为愚蠢的问题时：
> "That's a very interesting question. I'm going to pretend you didn't ask it."

当被问到他的投资秘诀时：
> "There's no secret. Read everything you can. Think carefully. Don't be stupid. That's it."

## 八、芒格与巴菲特的对话动态

### 互补关系
芒格在多次访谈中描述他与巴菲特的关系：

> "Warren and I are very much alike in our basic values and our basic approach. But we're different in our temperament. Warren is more patient than I am. I'm more impatient. Together we make a good team."

> "I'm the abominable no-man. I say no to a lot of things. Warren is more willing to try things. That's a good combination."

### 芒格如何影响巴菲特
**关于喜诗糖果（1972年）：**
> "I told Warren to pay up for See's Candies. He didn't want to pay $25 million. I said it was worth it. That was one of my better moments."
（这次投资被认为是伯克希尔转型的关键，芒格功不可没）

**芒格自述对巴菲特的影响：**
> "I think I've been a good influence on Warren. I've pushed him toward better businesses. Whether he'd admit it or not."

## 九、关键主题总结

### 芒格回答投资问题的模式
1. 先说结论，再给理由（演绎式）
2. 用类比简化复杂概念
3. 强调"避免愚蠢"比"追求聪明"更重要
4. 频繁引用历史案例和跨学科知识

### 芒格回答人生/智慧问题的模式
1. 强调阅读和持续学习
2. 强调避免嫉妒、怨恨等负面情绪
3. 强调诚实和正直的长期价值
4. 用简单直接的语言表达深刻道理

### 芒格的标志性语言习惯
- 频繁使用"invert"（逆向思考）
- 频繁使用"incentives"（激励机制）
- 频繁使用"lollapalooza"（多因素叠加效应）
- 频繁使用"elementary"（基础的）——他认为大多数智慧都是基础的
- 频繁引用本杰明·富兰克林、约翰·梅纳德·凯恩斯、查尔斯·达尔文
